Every now and again our local Mythology clubs meet up! We have meet and greets, and different clubs engage in different activities. The Egyptian Mythology clubs often volunteer at local animal shelters! Given that the Egyptians revered and worshipped cats, we often volunteer with cat shelters.

Fun fact: Washington D.C. has a cat cafe! It's a little cafe where you can get coffee or snacks, and there are cats everywhere for you to cuddle with! The cats are adoptable, and you can buy snacks to feed the cats.
