All of our incenses are hand-made with high quality raw ingredients. Our Kyphi Incense is based on a recipe recorded in hieroglyphs on the walls of Edfu Temple in Egypt. Kyphi was the most prized blend of incense in Ancient Egypt. In Heliopolis, Kyphi was burned in the evening to honour Ra and the Pyramid Texts (the world's oldest religious text) confirmed that the pharaoh would be able to enjoy Kyphi incense in the afterlife. The rest of our incenses are based on recipes found in Ancient Egyptian texts.

All ancient Egyptians wore jewellery. Its worth was not simply in its beauty, but in the meaning of the colours and designs used. While many of our designs are based on examples of Ancient Egyptian jewellery, we also have a modern collection inspired by the colours, materials, and techniques used at the time. All of our commercial jewellery is handmade.
